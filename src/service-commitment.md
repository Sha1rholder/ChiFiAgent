# Service Commitment

## Returns and Refunds

**Item Condition Issues**: If you bought a counterfeit or an item with obvious defects recognized by the trading platform, you can request an exchange or full refund.

**Change of Mind** (New Items Only): if you no longer want the item, you may return it within free return policy period (usually 7 days) after receipt. You will bear the return shipping cost and receive a refund minus a 5% restocking fee, capped at 200 CNY.

## Assistance and Responsibilities

**Warranty Claims**: Agent can assist with warranty claims for free, but you will pay any shipping costs related to repairs.

**Transparency**: Agent will proactively share seller communication records and purchase documentation to protect your rights as a consumer.

**Customs Duties**: Agent will make every effort to help you avoid customs duties, but you are responsible for duties imposed by customs.

**Logistics Choices**: The cross-border logistics quotation provided by Agent is a no-profit convenience reference. If you are not satisfied with it, you may ask Agent to use your own designated logistics carrier.

## Liability and Disclaimer

**Shipping Risks**: Cross-border transportation is outside Agent's direct control. There is a very low probability of serious delays, shipping damage, or lost parcels, and Agent is not responsible for those outcomes. If you want insurance, notify Agent before the parcel is dispatched, and Agent will provide a quote. If an insurance claim is paid out, Agent will refund the full compensation amount to you.

**Voltage Requirements**: Please inform Agent of your local voltage standard so the correct adapter can be selected. If the product becomes unusable or damaged because this information was not provided, Agent is not liable.

**Updates and Force Majeure**: Due to changes in international relations, tariff policies, and payment processor rules, this term may be updated without prior notice. However, barring force majeure, the proxy purchase terms are governed by the state at the time you place your order.
